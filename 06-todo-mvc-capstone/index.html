Lesson 06 — Todo MVC Capstone

This capstone combines two earlier patterns. From lesson 05 (MVC): the Model (model.js) owns the todo array and emits a todos-changed event on every mutation — it knows nothing about the DOM. The Views (<todo-input>, <todo-list>) only fire intent events and render what they are told — they know nothing about the Model. The Controller (controller.js) is the only layer that knows about both. From lesson 02 (event delegation): instead of attaching a listener to every checkbox and every remove button, <todo-list> has exactly one delegated click listener on its shadow root. It reads data-action and data-id off event.target to decide whether to dispatch a toggle or remove intent — adding a hundred todos still requires only that single listener.
